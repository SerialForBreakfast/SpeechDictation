Failure 001 - Event stream subscription order
================================================================================
Status
- Mitigated in code; needs device validation.

Notes
- Subscribe to engine.eventStream() before engine.start() so early events are not dropped.

Failure 002 - Timestamp resets on recognition restarts
================================================================================
Status
- Mitigated in LegacyTranscriptionEngine; needs long-session validation.

Notes
- Session-level offsets are applied to timing segments to keep timestamps monotonic.

Notes

Failure 005 - Transcript duplication at task boundaries
================================================================================
Status
- Mitigated 2026-03-14; needs device validation.

Notes
- Speech model can re-emit prior text when a new recognition task starts.
- TranscriptComposition strips suffix-overlap and deduplicates consecutive words.
- Applied in LegacyTranscriptionEngine, ModernTranscriptionEngine, SpeechRecognizer.
